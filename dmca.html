DMCA Policy

Effective date: 01/12/2026

Last updated: 01/12/2026

Plain-English summary

  • If you believe content infringes your copyright, notify us.
  • Provide the required details so we can act promptly.
  • We may remove content and notify the user.

1. Notice of infringement

If you believe your copyrighted work has been infringed, send a DMCA notice with the following information:

  • Your name, address, phone number, and email.
  • Identification of the copyrighted work.
  • The URL or location of the infringing material.
  • A statement of good faith belief that use is unauthorized.
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the notice is accurate.
  • Your physical or electronic signature.

2. Counter-notification

If you believe material was removed by mistake, you may submit a counter notice with your contact information, identification of the removed material, a statement under penalty of perjury, and consent to jurisdiction in your local federal court.

3. Repeat infringers

We may terminate accounts of users who are repeat infringers.

4. DMCA contact

Email DMCA notices to ASIInnovativeSolutions.llc@gmail.com and include “DMCA Notice” in the subject line.
