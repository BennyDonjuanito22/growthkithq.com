Security Overview

Effective date: 01/12/2026

Last updated: 01/12/2026

Plain-English summary

  • We use standard safeguards to protect data and systems.
  • We monitor for threats and respond to incidents quickly.
  • Report vulnerabilities to us for responsible handling.

1. Security measures

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Access controls and least-privilege practices.
  • Monitoring and logging for suspicious activity.
  • Regular backups and system updates.

2. Incident response

We investigate and respond to security events promptly and provide notices when required by law.

3. Your responsibilities

Protect your account credentials, enable available security controls, and report suspected compromise promptly.

4. Vulnerability reporting

Report security issues to ASIInnovativeSolutions.llc@gmail.com . Please include reproduction steps and relevant evidence. We do not permit destructive testing or service disruption.

5. Changes

We may update security practices as needed. The “Last updated” date reflects current information.
