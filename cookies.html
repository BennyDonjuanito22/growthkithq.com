1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device to enable website functionality and remember preferences.

2. Types of cookies we use

  • Essential cookies: Required for core functionality.
  • Analytics cookies (optional): Help us understand usage.

3. Analytics consent

Analytics cookies are only enabled after you accept them in the cookie banner. If you reject analytics, we do not load analytics scripts.

4. Managing preferences

You can update preferences using the “Manage” button in the cookie banner or by clearing cookies in your browser.

5. Retention

Cookie durations vary based on purpose. Essential cookies persist only as long as needed. Analytics cookies follow standard expiration practices.
